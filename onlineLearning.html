Best Online Learning Opportunities
The Learn Online portal promotes easy discovery of Ontario’s online learning opportunities by providing concise, at-your-fingertips information. We make it easy for you to locate the course or program of your choice, and to contact the institution that offers them, learners can access information on thousands of online courses and programs.

We are committed to offering learning experiences that are meaningful and rewarding for our students. We take pride in the courses that we offer and regularly evaluate the relevancy of course content while continuing to evolve with technology. Our commitment to providing exceptional online courses is reflected through our course design process and meets the high standards.

Each semester, we offer a wide selection of online courses from a variety of disciplines. Our online courses—both degree-credit and non-degree—make effective use of online technologies, multimedia, and external learning resources.
